About Us

What is Lost & Found Board?

Lost & Found Board is a simple platform designed to help members of a community report and recover lost items quickly and easily

What we do

  • Allow users to report lost items.
  • Allow users to post found items.
  • Help connect people with their belongings.
  • Make searching for items easier and faster.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make it easier for people in our community to recover lost items and return found belongings safely.

Contact

If you need help please contact us through the report page
